About this home

Welcome to this beautifully appointed home in the heart of Lake Nona, offering 4 bedrooms, a dedicated office, a spacious loft, a theater/media room, and the convenience of lawn care and internet included in the rent.

Designed with both comfort and functionality in mind, the open-concept floor plan features a bright and inviting living area that seamlessly connects to the dining space and chef-inspired kitchen. The kitchen is equipped with quartz countertops, a large center island, stainless steel appliances, a designer tile backsplash, and a generous walk-in pantry, making it perfect for everyday living and entertaining. Throughout the home, custom motorized blinds provide both style and convenience.

The primary suite is conveniently located on the first floor, creating a private retreat complete with an exceptionally large walk-in closet and a luxurious ensuite bathroom featuring dual quartz vanities and a glass-enclosed walk-in shower.

Also on the main level is a dedicated office, ideal for working from home, studying, or creating a quiet flex space, along with a well-equipped laundry room offering additional storage.

Upstairs, you'll find three generously sized secondary bedrooms, two full bathrooms, a spacious loft, and a dedicated theater/media room, providing flexible space for movie nights, a game room, playroom, or second living area. The home also offers abundant storage throughout.

Step outside and enjoy your private outdoor living space, where the backyard backs up to a beautiful natural conservation area, providing peaceful views and added privacy. Whether relaxing, grilling, or entertaining, you'll love the tranquil setting and Florida lifestyle.

Additional features include a spacious three-car garage with plenty of room for vehicles, storage, or hobbies.

Ideally located just minutes from Lake Nona Town Center, Medical City, Orlando International Airport, SR-417, top-rated dining, shopping, fitness centers, and entertainment, this home offers the perfect balance of convenience and community living. Residents also enjoy access to the exceptional amenities that make Laureate Park one of Central Florida's most sought-after neighborhoods.
