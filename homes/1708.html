About this home

Located in the heart of Lake Mary, this inviting 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom pool home offers a seamless blend of indoor and outdoor living. From the open foyer, enjoy a thoughtfully designed floor plan that flows effortlessly into the great room. The screened-in rear pool patio offers a private retreat for relaxing in the Florida sunshine or hosting friends and family. Just beyond the pool, a fully fenced lawn offers generous space for outdoor activities or simply unwinding in the open air.

Inside, the kitchen is both stylish and functional, featuring granite countertops, a modern tile backsplash, stainless steel appliances, and a dining nook.
